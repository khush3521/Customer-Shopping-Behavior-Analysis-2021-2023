#  Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis  
### Across Multiple Shopping Malls (2021–2023)

---

##  Project Overview
This capstone project focuses on analyzing **customer shopping behavior across multiple shopping malls** using transactional data from **2021 to 2023**.  
The project simulates a **real-world retail analytics scenario**, where raw data is transformed into meaningful insights to support **business decision-making**.

The analysis explores how customer behavior varies based on **gender, age group, product category, shopping mall, and payment method**, and how these factors influence **sales volume and revenue generation**.

---

##  Business Problem Statement
Retail businesses often face challenges in answering questions such as:
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
- How does shopping behavior differ across age groups and genders?
- Which payment methods drive high-value transactions?
- Are there seasonal patterns in customer spending?

This project aims to answer these questions using **data analytics and visualization techniques**.

---

##  Key Business Questions Addressed
- How is shopping distributed according to **gender**?
- Which gender did we sell more products to?
- Which gender generated more revenue?
- How is shopping distributed according to **age groups**?
- Which age group generated the highest revenue?
- How do **product categories** perform across malls and customer segments?
- Does the **payment method** have a relationship with revenue and demographics?
- What are the **seasonal trends** in sales performance?

---

##  Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional shopping data with the following attributes:

- Invoice Number  
- Customer ID  
- Gender  
- Age  
- Product Category  
- Quantity  
- Price  
- Payment Method (Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card)  
- Invoice Date (2021–2023)  
- Shopping Mall Name  

The data represents purchases made by customers from various demographic groups across multiple shopping malls.

---

##  Data Preparation & Transformation
Data preparation was performed to ensure accuracy and usability:

- Checked for duplicate and missing values  
- Created calculated fields such as **Total Amount (Price × Quantity)**  
- Extracted **Year and Month** from invoice dates  
- Created **Age Groups** for demographic analysis  
- Standardized data types using Power Query  

These steps ensured the dataset was **clean, structured, and analysis-ready**.

##  Key Insights
- Revenue shows **clear seasonal patterns** across months and years  
- **Credit card transactions generate higher revenue per transaction**  
- Cash dominates transaction volume but contributes lower average order value  
- Customer behavior varies significantly by **age group and gender**  
- A small number of categories contribute a large share of total revenue  
- Mall performance differs based on category mix and customer demographics  

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Promote **credit card and digital payment offers** to increase high-value purchases  
- Run **seasonal marketing campaigns** during low-revenue months  
- Focus inventory planning on **high-performing categories and malls**  
- Use **customer segmentation** to design targeted promotions  
- Encourage digital payment adoption to improve average order value
